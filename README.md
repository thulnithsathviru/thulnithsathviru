## 👋 About Me

Undergraduate in **Urban Informatics & Planning** at the University of Moratuwa, passionate about shaping sustainable, efficient, and resilient cities. I turn raw geographic data into interactive visualizations, clear maps, and strategic insight for urban and environmental projects.

- 🎓 &nbsp;**BSc (Hons) Urban Informatics & Planning** — University of Moratuwa (2023 – 2027)
- 💼 &nbsp;**Planning Intern** @ Keells (John Keells Holdings)
- 🌍 &nbsp;**Freelance GIS Analyst** since 2023 — spatial analytics & data visualization for global clients
- 🧭 &nbsp;Foundation in urban design, land management, transportation planning, spatial data analytics & environmental sustainability
- 🎨 &nbsp;Certified in Graphic Design
- 📫 &nbsp;Reach me at **thulnithsathviruofficial@gmail.com**
